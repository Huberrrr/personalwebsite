Pulse Voter Representative Site
HTML
CSS
JavaScript
React JS
Node.js
SQL

While interning at Pulse Voter, I was tasked with re-creating their website that elected officials were to use. Representatives currently use this site to view bills currently in Congress and analyze voters in their district. I redeveloped the site from the ground up, and added more features including: a restful API, a customized homepage, options for representatives to export information about a bill and its votes to a CSV file, a Google Map showing all the voters in their district and their information/voting history, and in-depth statistics on specific bills.

Halo Wars 2 Statistics Discord Bot
JavaScript
Node.js

As a side project during my second semester of college, I decided that I wanted to explore the world of Discord (an online chat service centered around gaming) and its chat bots. I found a game that didn't have any existing statistics bot, Halo Wars 2, and I created one for the community. The bot provides players their statistics in every gamemode, and even compiles their data to give them an overall look at their career for the game. It currently averages around 1000 users in 10 active communities.
